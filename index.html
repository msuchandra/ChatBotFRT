Some EdTech Questions.

What is Edtech?

Edtech is a combination of the words education and technology, and refers to the use of technology to enhance teaching and learning experiences.

What are some examples of Edtech tools?

Some examples of Edtech tools include learning management systems, video conferencing software, interactive whiteboards, online quizzes and assessments, and educational apps.

How does Edtech benefit students?

Edtech can benefit students by making learning more engaging, accessible, and personalized. It can also provide opportunities for students to collaborate with peers and access resources outside of the classroom.

How does Edtech benefit teachers?

Edtech can benefit teachers by providing tools to help them save time, streamline administrative tasks, and better track student progress. It can also offer new ways to deliver content and engage with students.
